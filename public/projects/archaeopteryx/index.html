Archaeopteryx

what 🍪 Custom MIDI hardware
when 🍪 2011
A DIY MIDI controller hacked together from two Korg Nanokontrols, a QWERTY keyboard, and a wooden cutlery box. Archaeopteryx was designed with a DJ-style A/B mixer configuration in mind and hooked into VDMX was used for long duration VJ performances.
