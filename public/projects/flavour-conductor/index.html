The Flavour Conductor

what 🍪 Software for interactive pipe-organ performance
with 🍪 Bompas and Parr 🍪 Johnny Walker
when 🍪 2014
where 🍪 Merchant Taylors' Hall, London

I provided software to interface Ableton Live with a pipe organ built for Johnny Walker. The organ was unveiled at Symphony in Blue, an immersive theatrical experience put together by Bompas and Parr. The software was branched and customized from plugins developed for interfacing with The Melbourne Town Hall Grand Organ in 2013.
